Our story

It started with three tanks and a lot of bad advice from forums.

Brightfin started the way most fishkeeping obsessions do — a ten-gallon tank, a few neon tetras, and a slow slide into 4am scrolling about water parameters. What we couldn't find anywhere was a source that told us the truth about a fish before we bought it: where it actually came from, how it was raised, what it really needed.

So we built that. We work directly with breeders we've vetted in person, quarantine everything ourselves, and never carry wild-caught fish from non-sustainable fisheries. If we wouldn't put it in our own tanks, it doesn't go in yours.
